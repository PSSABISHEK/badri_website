About Me

Hello! I’m Badrinarayan Mohan and I’m an avid technology and Esports enthusiast, started loving to code by seeing how aesthetic it is. Ive always loved playing games since I was 11 years old. Internship

AIESEC Sri Lanka

Global Volunteer English Teacher• December 2016 – January 2017

AIESEC is the world's largest non-profit youth-run organization. It is an international non-governmental not-for-profit organization that provides young people with leadership development, cross-cultural global internships, and volunteer exchange experiences across the globe. The organization focuses on empowering young people to make a positive impact on society. The AIESEC network includes approximately 27,000 members in 127 countries. I had been to Sri Lanka for a two month long winter internship where I volunteered to teach 1–10th grade kids in KEA International School, Kantale.
